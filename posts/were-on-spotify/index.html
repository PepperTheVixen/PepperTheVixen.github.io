We're on Spotify!

Hell yes

October 27, 2022

You may have noticed a shiny new link on the homepage. Yup, we’re on Spotify now. Apple Music and iTunes, too. Amazon music is lagging, but we’ll get there. If you’d rather buy my stuff outright, I’ll be posting on Bandcamp as well!

Follow me on Spotify Folow me on Bandcamp

I’m super excited to finally be on the major music stores. It means that I can get my music to more people. Sure, I’ll make some money off it, but I’m not counting on raking it in. Not when each listen gets me a tenth of a cent or something. In any case, I’ve got a distributor (Distrokid), I’ve got a Spotify page, and I’ve got music. Time to bring on the floof.
