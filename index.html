About me

Hi! Names Pepper. I’m an electronic musician in love with everything from synthwave to EDM. If it’s got synths, I probably like it. I make music and stream games on Twitch. Fun fact: I do it all totally blind. Curious how? Come along for the ride and find out! I’ve got all sorts of neat tricks up my sleeve. I also love to chat about what I do, so feel free to ask away!
